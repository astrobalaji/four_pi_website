What is 4pi Astronomics?

At 4pi Astronomics we are committed to two goals:
a) To promote the systematic collaborations between astrophysicists, amateur astronomers and educators in the domains of scientific data acquisition and knowledge transfer.
b) To increase access to Astronomy for broader audiences by dramatically reducing the costs of telescopes, accessories and observatories using 3D-printing and production decentralization methods.

What does 4pi Astronomics offer?

Astrophysicists

4pi offers researchers access to low-cost, high time resolution data at their convenience. No more proposals, deadlines, selection committees and risk of rejections.

Astrophysicists

Secure low-cost observational data anytime.

1

Easy access to high time resolution data

Tired of waiting for call-for-proposals periods of different telescopes, time-consuming proposal preparations and strict submission deadlines? Through our global network of amateur astronomers you can have easy, affordable and on-demand access to high time resolution photometric data anytime with a few clicks.
2

Take control of your data inflow

Is your data inflow interrupted by rejections of your proposed science cases? Through our global network of 0.1 - 0.4 meter telescopes you can request and secure data whenever you wish, either to boost your research productivity and publication rates, or to simply better support your future proposals. 
3

Be always up-to-speed  

Why wait until the next conference to present you latest results? Through our online platform you can share your latest scientific results with the scientific community instantly, receive useful feedback through fruitful discussions and expand your network with researchers around the world. 
4

Benefit from your research outreach

Through our online platform, you can share your scientific expertise with amateur astronomers and students around the world, by providing educational material, lectures and/or masterclasses to our network. This way we can improve our services to you and in return you are rewarded with discounted data acquisition rates. 

Amateur Astronomers

Become a Citizen-Scientist at the forefront of cutting-edge Astronomy research

1

Become a Citizen-Scientist

Why not be a part of a new scientific discovery or help answer key questions about the Universe? Through our online platform you can join forces with astrophysicists around the world by collecting observational data for their research projects. All you need is a telescope, an RGB filter set, an EQ tracker mount and a good quality CCD/CMOS camera. 
2

Earn while you practice your favorite hobby

Why does your expensive equipment have to sit idle and not provide extra income for you? Based on the time you are willing to spend on observing astronomical targets for our network's data acquisition activities, you will be rewarded with monetary and many more benefits. 
3

Sharpen-up your understanding of the Cosmos

Wish to evolve your understanding of the Cosmos beyond what planetariums and public talks can offer? Through our online platform you can achieve this by having access to educational material and/or attend lectures and masterclasses from astrophysicists around the world

Educators

Introduce your students to Astronomy and the scientific process

1

Connect your pupils with Astronomy

Involve your students with Astronomy through educational material and lectures from the world's leading astrophysicists. 
2

Bringing the night sky to your classroom

Organize live streams of the night sky to your classroom from everywhere in the world, through our network's amateur telescope stations. 
3

Turn your students to scientists

Does your institution have a telescope? By joining our network, you can organize your students in teams and guide them through the process of scientific data acquisition and analysis.

What are we planning?

An online Astronomy community

An online platform where astrophysicists, amateur astronomers and educators around the world can interact and collaborate with each other on many different levels.

An innovative telescope

We are developing a unique, smart telescope with innovative non-glass based optics. Our vision is to provide telescopes that are light, strong, modular and affordable, while maintaining high optical quality standards.

An IoT based backyard observatory

We are developing a low-cost, IoT, smart telescope housing, ideal for backyards and roof-tops. Easy to assemble, it turns your home into an observatory ready for operation within minutes.
